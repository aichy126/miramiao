Slice any photo into rainbow strips and turn it into a wallpaper.

Contact

Questions, bugs, or feature ideas? Email info@miramiao.com and we’ll get back to you. Telling us your device model and iOS version helps a lot.

Frequently asked

Where are my wallpapers saved?

Tap Export and the wallpaper is saved straight to your photo library. From there, set it as your wallpaper in the iOS Settings app (Settings → Wallpaper).

Why does Prism ask for photo and camera permission?

Photo access lets Prism save the wallpapers you generate back to your library. Camera access is only used if you choose to take a new photo instead of picking an existing one. Everything is processed on your device — nothing is uploaded. See our Privacy Policy.

I bought Pro but it isn’t unlocked

Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple account you bought with. If it still doesn’t unlock, email us.

Does Prism need an internet connection?

No. Prism works fully offline — it contains no networking code at all. Only the App Store purchase itself goes through Apple.

Can I use several photos in one wallpaper?

Yes. Pick multiple photos and assign them across the slices, then drag and pinch each one to adjust how it sits inside its slice.
