Privacy Policy for Prism

Last updated: 2026-07-16

This Privacy Policy describes how miramiao (“we”, “us”) handles your information when you use Prism (“the app”).

Data Collection

Prism does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no accounts. It does not contain any networking code — there are no servers of ours that could receive your data, and we share nothing with third parties.

Your Photos

Prism reads the photos you explicitly choose (or take with the camera) and turns them into wallpapers. All processing happens entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere. The app only asks for permission to save the wallpapers you generate back to your photo library, and to use the camera if you choose to take a new photo.

Purchases

If you buy Prism Pro, the purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Your purchase status is kept on your device and in your Apple account so it can be restored — we do not receive any personal information from it.

Tracking

Prism does not track you. It does not use the advertising identifier (IDFA), does not link data to your identity, and does not share data with other companies for advertising or analytics.

Children’s Privacy

Prism is not directed at children under 13, and since the app collects no data at all, we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@miramiao.com.
